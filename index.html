Care for your candles

A few simple habits will help every Candel candle burn cleaner, safer, and longer.

  1. Trim before every burn

    Keep your wick around 5mm to reduce soot and help the flame stay steady.

  2. Let the wax reach the edges

    On the first few burns, let the melt pool reach the sides of the glass to prevent tunneling.

  3. Stop with 1cm of wax left

    For safety, extinguish your candle when there is about 1cm of wax remaining in the jar.
