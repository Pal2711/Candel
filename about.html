Our story

Candles that feel like home.

Candel began with a simple idea: light that slows you down and scents that tell a story.

Made in small batches, not warehouses.

Every Candel candle is hand‑poured in our small studio using natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and custom fragrance blends. We design each scent to feel balanced—present in the room, never overpowering.

Our team tests burn performance, scent throw, and vessel temperature so you can simply light, breathe, and enjoy.

Between every pour we take notes, adjust ratios, and revisit burn tests so that the candle on your shelf feels as considered as the first prototype we ever made.

How we pour a Candel candle

A simple, careful process from wax to warm glow.

  1. Blend

    We gently heat soy wax and combine it with custom fragrance oils, measuring every batch by hand.

  2. Pour

    Each vessel is wicked, checked, and poured in small batches for a smooth, even finish.

  3. Cure

    Candles cure for several days to allow the fragrance to bind to the wax, improving scent throw.

What guides every Candel

Beyond ingredients and process, our values shape how every candle is designed, poured, and shipped.

Ritual over rush

We create scents that encourage you to slow down, mark small moments, and come back to yourself.

Considered sourcing

From wax to glassware, we look for partners who prioritize transparency and lower‑impact materials.

Longevity

Our vessels are meant to be reused—display stems, store pens, or keep them on your shelf long after the last burn.
